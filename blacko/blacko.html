Bio

Black Sherif is a fast-rising Ghanaian musician who seems to be taking over the music space with his released freestyle 'The Second Sermon'

Known in real life as Mohammed Ismail Sherif, the 19-year-old musician has evolved from a viral sensation to one of Ghana’s hottest music stars pushing Hip Hop and Trap Music to new heights in just one year.

The artiste who has become a threat to poverty according to many Ghanaian music lovers came through the limelight with his major ‘Second Sermon’ hit song.
